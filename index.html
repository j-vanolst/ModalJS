Welcome to modal.js

modal.js is an interactive multimedia application designed to teach people about the various musical modes. This application will look at the modes of the major scale - the musical scale most people are familiar with.

A musical mode is a unique combination of notes with a distinct 'flavour' that characterizes the feeling or sound of a musical piece.

The seven modes of the major scale are as follows:

  • Ionian
  • Dorian
  • Phrygian
  • Lydian
  • Mixolydian
  • Aeolian
  • Locrian

You can click on a mode from the list to learn more about it or you can navigate through the site using the navigation menu.
The try it out page has an interactive piano/keyboard where you can apply ideas you may have learned from the information pages.
